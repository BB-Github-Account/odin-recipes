Christmas Cookies

Description

A great recipe for a fresh batch of soft christmas cookies. Don't forget you can also top with frosting after the cookies have had some time to cool or sprinkle on some sugar right before you bake. For the best results it's important to remember to mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl, chill the dough in the fridge before baking, and to bake the cookies until the edges are golden.

Ingredients

  • 3 ¾ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup margarine, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium sized bowl, set aside for later.
  2. In separate large bowl beat the sugar and margarine with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Beat eggs into the margarine mixture along side the vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the contents of the first bowl that was set aside in step 1 with the larger bowl and stir until dough is formed.
  5. Wrap dough and let it chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  6. After dough has finished chilling, preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C), and grease to cookie sheets.
  7. Roll dough on a floured surface to 1/4 inches in thickness and cut out shapes with cookie cutters.
  8. Bake for 6 to 8 minutes or until edges are golden and remember to let the cookies cool before eating.
